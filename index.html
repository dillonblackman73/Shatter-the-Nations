About Us

A national movement designed to reach, connect, and restore teenagers at a moment when isolation, anxiety, and spiritual confusion are defining an entire generation.

Here’s the reality:
Teens are asking life-and-death questions every day — and too often, no one is answering them.

Our solution is not theoretical. It is operational.

Our System

Shatter the Nations combines three proven pillars into one scalable system:

  • Nationwide teen conferences that clearly present hope, truth, and purpose
  • A global digital platform that provides ongoing encouragement, spiritual guidance, and mental-health check-ins
  • Localized intervention, connecting teens directly to vetted churches, counselors, and trusted advisors in their own communities

When a teen reaches out for help, they are not left alone. They are immediately connected to real people, real resources, and real follow-through.

Our Goal

  • Train and onboard churches, counselors, and advisors across the country

This is not a short-term program. It is an infrastructure for the next generation. If you’ve ever asked, “What can actually turn the tide for young people?” — this is it.
